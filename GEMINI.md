# Project: Industrial RFQ Engine
## Goal
A B2B SaaS app where industrial companies upload messy PDF/Email RFQs.
Our system extracts the data and generates a clean PDF Quote.

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: Next.js (App Router), Tailwind CSS, Lucide Icons.
- Backend: Supabase (Auth & DB).
- AI Engine: n8n Webhook.

## Vibe
- "Boring but Professional" industrial aesthetic.
- Colors: Dark Slate Grey, Safety Orange (for buttons), White.
- Font: Inter or Roboto.
- Dense data tables (like Excel but cleaner).
